1. Description of Service

Portfolio Agent is an AI-powered portfolio analysis tool. It allows you to connect your brokerage account (via a third-party connection provider), ask questions about your portfolio in natural language, and receive analysis such as allocation breakdowns, performance metrics, and hypothetical “what-if” scenarios. The Service is read-only: it does not execute trades or move funds.

3. Data and Privacy

We take privacy and security seriously:

  • We do not store your brokerage login credentials. Connection to your brokerage is handled by a secure third-party provider (SnapTrade). We never see or store your brokerage username or password.
  • Brokerage connection data (e.g. tokens used to access your account in read-only mode) is encrypted using industry-standard encryption (AES-256-GCM) with keys derived per user.
  • We do not store your portfolio holdings, balances, or transaction history in our database. Holdings are fetched when you ask a question and cached only briefly in memory (e.g. 60 seconds) to improve performance.
  • Chat messages are encrypted at rest. When you are signed in, your chat history is stored server-side with AES-256-GCM encryption using a unique key derived from your user ID. Each message is encrypted individually. No one—including our team—can read your stored conversations without access to both the database and the server-side encryption keys.
  • Portfolio data is anonymized before reaching the AI. When you ask a question, your holdings are fetched from SnapTrade, but only allocation percentages, asset classes, and ticker symbols are sent to the AI. Dollar amounts, share counts, cost basis, and account balances are stripped on our server and never reach Anthropic. The AI works with percentages only.
  • Chat content is sent to Anthropic (Claude) for AI responses. Anthropic's API data policy states that API inputs and outputs are not used to train their models. Anthropic may retain API inputs and outputs for up to 30 days for trust and safety purposes (e.g. to detect abuse), after which they are automatically deleted. Because we anonymize portfolio data before sending it, Anthropic never sees your actual financial figures. For full details, see Anthropic's Privacy Policy and Usage Policy.
  • If enabled, chat content may also be sent to observability tools (e.g. Langfuse, LangSmith) for performance monitoring, in accordance with their respective policies.

For more detail, see our Privacy documentation.
