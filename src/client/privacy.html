Privacy

What we store

We store your account identifier (Supabase user ID), email (if provided), and—when you connect a brokerage—encrypted connection data (SnapTrade user secret and institution name). Brokerage connection secrets are encrypted per-user with AES-256-GCM.

Chat history: When you are signed in, your chat messages are stored server-side and encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM with a unique encryption key derived from your user ID. Each message is encrypted individually. This means your conversations cannot be read by anyone without access to both the database and the server-side encryption keys. Chats are limited to 50 messages each; older messages are automatically removed. You can delete any chat at any time.

We do not store your brokerage login credentials, portfolio holdings, balances, or transaction history in our database.

What we don’t store

Portfolio holdings and prices are fetched when you ask questions and cached only briefly in memory (e.g. 60 seconds). We never see or store your brokerage username or password.

Third parties

Anthropic (Claude): Your chat messages are sent to Anthropic’s API to generate AI responses. Per Anthropic’s data policy, API inputs and outputs are not used to train their models. Anthropic may retain API data for up to 30 days for trust and safety purposes (abuse detection), after which it is automatically deleted. See Anthropic’s Privacy Policy.

SnapTrade: Brokerage data is sent to SnapTrade to retrieve your holdings in read-only mode.

Observability (optional): If enabled, chat content may be sent to tools such as Langfuse or LangSmith for performance monitoring.

See our Terms of Service for more.
